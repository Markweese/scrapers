There are a lot of crazy ultra-events out there, but this one might take the cake. The DECA Ironman is a race to finish 10 Ironman-distance triathlons in 10 days and  the fastest cumulative time wins.

Let us break it out for you what the DECA Ironman actually entails,

24 miles of swimming

1,120 miles of biking

262 miles of running
